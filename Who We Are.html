SEE THE TEAM BEHIND THE SCENES

Meet the team behind the scenes working to keep The Coventry Charity Store up and running, working tirelessly to raise money through the store and hosting fundraising events.

WHO ARE WE?

We are an independent store located in Coventry working with a range of partners and charities to provide and excellent service

As part of the community we are everyone, we are friends, family and the local community. We were established in 2008 and have since been awarded many accredited certificates and awards for our contribution with the local community and overseas

Come in store and say "Hello" and come see who we are, what we are about. We strive to provide and excellent service and help in anyway we can
...MEET THE TEAM

Our team is made up of pad and non-paid hard working volunteers within the local community, we strive to provide as many benefits as possible for our employees although we are a non for profit organization which allows us to provide as much money in charity as possible

We are currently recruiting volunteers to work weekends and be flexible, if you are interested in giving back to the community Email us a copy of your CV and someone will be in touch
